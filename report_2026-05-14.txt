  (24h, with back overlap via deadlift and lat pulldown), Mon→Tue (24h), and

  Repeated sessions before muscles fully recover add cumulative fatigue rather
  than new stimulus, and mask real strength. Where the schedule allows, insert
  a full rest day between sessions — especially before heavy compound days.

  counterbalancing the larger chest and front shoulder. When pressing muscles

  This week rear delts logged 7 sets against an 8-set minimum (1 short). Traps
