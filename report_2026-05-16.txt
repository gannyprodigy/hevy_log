  pressing muscles (anterior deltoid, chest) dominate over the rear delts, the
  shoulder joint gets pulled slightly forward under load — over months this
  creates impingement and rotator cuff tears. This week rear delts hit 7 sets vs
  an 8-set target: one set short. The good news is Face Pull e1RM has progressed
  from 36.3kg (21 Apr) to 39.8kg now — steady improvement. Traps are excellent
  at 16 sets against a 6-set target. Action: add one Face Pull set on Pull day
  (4 sets instead of 3) to consistently hit the 8-set rear delt minimum.
